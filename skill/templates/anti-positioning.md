# Anti-Positioning Guide Template

## Questions to Ask

1. **Roles to avoid:** What kind of work do you NOT want? (management, certain industries, company stages)
2. **Experience to downplay:** What past roles/companies shouldn't you lead with? Why?
3. **Red flags:** What phrases in job postings make you immediately skip?
4. **Interview deflections:** Any topics you want to redirect if asked?
5. **Compensation floor:** What's your minimum? Currency?
6. **Positioning:** How do you WANT to be seen? What's your 2-sentence pitch?

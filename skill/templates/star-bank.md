# STAR Story Bank Template

## Questions to Ask

Gather 4-6 stories by asking:

1. **Biggest achievement:** What's the accomplishment you're most proud of?
2. **Speed/execution:** Tell me about a time you shipped something fast.
3. **Scale/growth:** Have you driven measurable growth? Users, revenue, efficiency?
4. **Failure/learning:** Tell me about a time something didn't work out.
5. **Leadership:** When did you lead a team or initiative through difficulty?
6. **Technical depth:** A time you got hands-on with implementation?

For each story, extract: Situation, Task, Action (specific steps), Result (with metrics).
